HORUS — PRODUCT ROADMAP
=========================
Updated: 2026-07-01

THREE LAYERS (BUILD SEQUENCE)
-----------------------------

Layer 1 — Document ingestion (MVP)
- Parse PH bank statements (BDO, BPI, Metrobank, Landbank, UnionBank, rural banks) from PDFs, scans, photos
- Extract sales ledgers, inventory notes where possible
- Moat: OCR that works on messy PH formats — confirmed hardest problem (ewb-oil-crisis-stress-test-v1 · mom at EWB: "definitely OCR")

Layer 2 — Structured reconstruction
- Map extractions → income / expense / debt streams (2–3 year horizon per Dindo)
- Dynamic rows (utilities, outsourced labor, loan facilities, household expenses)
- Analyst reviews/corrects — productivity tool, not decisioning (horus-competitive-landscape · BSP Circular 855 / 1137 framing)

Layer 3 — Judgment augmentation
- Industry seasonality prompts (eggs/school holidays, resorts, agri)
- Business-model identification (contract grower vs commercial, in-house motorcycle lending)
- ESDG / heat-map checklist capture
- Multi-business consolidation + intercompany adjustments

Platform wrapper
- Case management: active apps, stage, missing docs
- Aggregate exposure lookup automation (Call 2 pain — simpler win)
